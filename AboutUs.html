About Us

We are a regional airline with route networks covering major local and regional cities, Hippo plays a significant role in the country’s hospitality, travel and tourism industry and is vital contributor to the country’s socio-economic development. The airline’s head office is based in Cape Town

Purpose

An integrated, sustainable regional airline connecting secondary and main airports.

Vission

To be a sustainable world-class regional airline with an extensive footprint in Africa.

Core Values

  • We never compromise on safety, no matter what.
  • Our customers are our most important investors.
  • We partner with people across all operations.
  • We deliver with speed without compromising on quality.
  • We strive for continuous improvement.
  • We keep it simple.
